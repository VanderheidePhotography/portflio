About Me

My Name is Brandon Vanderheide. I am an independent photographer and videographer with a passion for capturing the most meaningful moments in life on this beautiful planet. Whether it’s a wedding, event, or a simple portrait session, I am dedicated to preserving memories that you’ll cherish forever. In addition to working with clients, I enjoy shooting wildlife, street photography, and astrophotography, which fuels my creative vision. I also specialize in real estate, architectural, and automotive photography, helping your listings stand out and receive the recognition they deserve. I work exclusively with high-quality, professional equipment, including the Fujifilm XT-5 and a selection of premium lenses. This ensures that every shot I take is crisp, vibrant, and exactly aligned with your vision. Whether you're looking to showcase a product, capture a personal milestone, or create stunning visuals for your business, I am committed to delivering exceptional results that will exceed your expectations. If you'd like to collaborate or learn more about my work, feel free to reach out via email or phone. Let's bring your vision to life!
